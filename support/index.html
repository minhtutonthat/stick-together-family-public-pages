Frequently asked questions
How do I invite family members?

Go to Settings → Family → Invite Code. Share the 6-character code with your family members. They can enter it in the app when joining a family group.

Can multiple people use the app at the same time?

Yes. All data is synced in real time via our cloud. Multiple family members can add tasks, check off shopping items or write diary entries simultaneously.

What does the subscription include?

The subscription unlocks the full app for your entire family group. A single subscription covers all members — everyone benefits once one person subscribes.

  • Unlimited tasks, shopping lists and diary entries
  • Google Drive integration and file attachments
  • Finance & net worth tracking
  • Virtual family pet with AI riddles
My Google Drive connection isn't working. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Go to Settings → Google and tap Disconnect, then reconnect.
  • Make sure you have granted all requested permissions when signing in with Google.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • If the problem persists, contact us at tonthat.consulting@gmail.com.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

How is my data stored and kept private?

Your family data is stored securely in our cloud database and is only accessible to members of your family group. Files and photos are stored in your own Google Drive account. We never sell or share your data with third parties for advertising

How do I delete my account or family?

To delete a family, go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Family. This permanently removes all family data (admin only).
