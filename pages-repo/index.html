Privacy Policy

Stick Together Family  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters. Stick Together Family is designed for families and we are committed to keeping your personal data safe and never selling it to anyone.

1. Who We Are

Stick Together Family ("we", "our", "the app") is developed and maintained by Minh Tu TON THAT. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: minhtutonthat@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information: email address and password (used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase).
  • Family content: data you create inside the app — tasks, shopping lists, recipes, diary entries, memos, contacts, calendar events, finance records, and notes. This data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to members of your family group.
  • Photos and files: images you add to diary entries or memos, and files you attach to tasks or shopping projects. These are stored in your Google Drive account (via Google Drive integration) or in our secure storage, depending on your setup.
  • Music/audio files: MP3 files you upload are stored in your Google Drive account under a dedicated folder.
  • Device information: basic device identifiers used by RevenueCat to manage your subscription status. No personally identifiable device data is shared.
  • Subscription data: purchase receipts and subscription status managed by RevenueCat (Apple App Store / Google Play). We do not store your payment card information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To sync your family's data across devices.
  • To manage access to premium features via subscription.
  • To generate AI-powered riddles for the virtual pet feature (content sent to OpenRouter API — no personal data is included in these requests).
  • To send you calendar reminders and task notifications (on-device only, no marketing emails).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted services, strictly to operate the app:

  • Supabase – database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Drive API – file and photo storage in your own Google Drive account (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat – subscription and in-app purchase management (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenRouter / AI providers – anonymous requests to generate riddle content for the virtual pet. No user-identifiable data is sent.

5. Google Drive Integration

If you choose to connect Google Drive, the app requests permission to read and write files inside a specific "Stick Together Family" folder in your Drive. We do not access any files outside that folder. You can revoke this permission at any time via your Google Account settings.

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Your family's data in Supabase is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies, meaning only authenticated members of your family can access your family's data. No other family's data is ever accessible to you.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted from our systems. Family content (shared with other members) may persist until all members remove it.

8. Children's Privacy

Stick Together Family is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Family members under 13 should use the app only under parental supervision.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent (e.g. disconnect Google Drive).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at minhtutonthat@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

10. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not handle or store payment information. Subscription status is tracked via RevenueCat and shared at the family level — one subscription covers all family members. The first 30 days after a family is created are free without requiring a subscription.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
