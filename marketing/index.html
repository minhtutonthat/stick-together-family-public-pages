built for real family life

From daily chores to precious memories — Stick Together Family covers everything your household needs.

✅

Tasks & Checklists

Create and assign tasks to family members. Track deadlines, add notes and attach files. Never forget who's responsible for what.

🛒

Shopping Lists & Projects

Build shared shopping lists with your family. Reference a master item list, organise by project and check things off together in real time.

📅

Family Calendar

One calendar for everyone. Add events, set reminders and sync with your device calendar so nothing slips through the cracks.

🍳

Recipes & Cooking

Save your family's favourite recipes. Generate a shopping list directly from any recipe with a single tap.

📔

Diary with Photos

Capture family memories in a shared diary. Add rich text, inline photos and attachments to preserve every moment.

📝

Memos & Contacts

Store important notes, family contacts and documents — all organised and always accessible to every member.

💰

Finance & Net Worth

Track your household finances, monitor net worth over time and stay on top of your family's financial goals.

🐟

Virtual Family Pet

Raise a virtual pet together! Feed it, keep it healthy and solve AI-generated riddles as a family. When the pet graduates, start all over again.

☁️

Google Drive Integration

Attach files, photos and documents directly from your Google Drive. Everything stays linked and migratable — even if you switch accounts.
